System Workflow

🌾

Field Monitoring

Stage 1 - Maturity Detection

Farmer uses handheld device with ESP32 and stainless steel probes. Extracts juice sample and measures conductivity. Device displays maturity status.

✂️

Harvest Decision

Stage 2 - Ready for Action

Once maturity confirmed, status shows "Harvest Ready". Farmer proceeds with harvesting, preparing for immediate transport.

📱

Transport Request

Stage 3 - Mobile App

Farmer opens mobile app and requests transport truck. App displays available trucks nearby with distance and arrival time.

🚛

Truck Matching

Stage 4 - Smart Coordination

Select a truck below! Driver receives notification and accepts pickup. System confirms assignment with arrival time.

Mill Delivery

Stage 5 - Sugar Mill

Truck loads harvested sugarcane and transports directly to mill, ensuring fresh delivery and reducing sucrose loss.
