Complete System Workflow

🌾

Field Monitoring

Stage 1 - Maturity Detection

Farmer uses handheld device with ESP32, two stainless steel probes, and conductivity circuit. Extracts juice sample and measures electrical conductivity. Device displays maturity status and sends result to mobile app.

✂️

Harvest Decision

Stage 2 - Ready for Action

Once maturity is confirmed, crop status shows "Harvest Ready". Farmer proceeds with harvesting the sugarcane, preparing it for immediate transport to ensure maximum freshness and minimize sucrose degradation.

📱

Transport Request

Stage 3 - Mobile App

After harvesting, farmer opens the mobile app and clicks "Request Transport Truck". The app displays available trucks nearby with distance, estimated arrival time, and truck capacity information. Farmer can select based on preferences.

🚛

Truck Matching & Assignment

Stage 4 - Smart Coordination

Try selecting a truck below! Available trucks show with live distances. Once you select, the driver receives notification, accepts pickup, and system confirms assignment with arrival time. Real-time tracking begins.

🏭

Mill Delivery

Stage 5 - Sugar Mill

Truck arrives at field and loads the harvested sugarcane. Cane is transported directly to the sugar mill ensuring fresh delivery within hours. Fresh cane reduces sucrose loss and optimizes sugar mill processing efficiency.
